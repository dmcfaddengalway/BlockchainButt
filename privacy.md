# Privacy Policy

**Blockchain Butt** is a Chrome extension that replaces the word "blockchain" with "buttcrack" on web pages.

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. All text replacement is performed locally within your browser. No information of any kind is sent to any server or third party.

The `activeTab` permission is used solely to access the text content of the current page for the purpose of replacing words. This data never leaves your device.
